Emon Pulo Beach Resort

San Antonio, Zambales

Tucked away along the pristine shores of Zambales, Emon Pulo Beach Resort offers a unique and secluded island getaway. With its stretch of powdery sand beach, clear waters, and lush tropical surroundings, Emon Pulo provides a serene atmosphere that leaves guests awestruck.

Located on an island in Silanguin Cove, the place offers exclusivity and privacy to guests. Accessible only by boat, Emon Pulo promises a respite in paradise, away from crowded tourist destinations. Transportation to the resort is made convenient with boat pick-up services.

The resort’s elegant villas and cottages provide the perfect blend of modern comforts and traditional Filipino architecture. Envisioned to be a modern Filipino-style tropical beach resort with a touch of home, Emon Pulo’s landscape and accommodations were designed with high regard for comfort, safety, and class.

Emon Pulo in Zambales is more than just a destination; it is an opportunity for guests to unwind and reconnect with nature. This is one of the places you find yourself planning to go back to even before you leave.
